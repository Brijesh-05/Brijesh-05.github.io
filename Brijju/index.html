About CMRIT:

CMR Institute of Technology is a private engineering college located in Bengaluru, India. CMRIT is a technical and management institution affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. CMRIT is recognized by Government of Karnataka.
